Secondary buttons

20. FINAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES
The 5 Rules:

Gradient = Attention
Only use it where you want users to click/interact.
Whitespace > Color
Let breathing room do the work.
Less is More
Resist the urge to add gradient everywhere.
Consistency Builds Trust
Use same gradient formula every time.
Logo Anchors the Brand
Let the logo carry the color story. Design stays clean.
